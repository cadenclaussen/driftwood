# Crafting Recipes

All craftable items and their requirements. Recipes unlock after obtaining the key material.

## Basic Processing

| Recipe | Materials | Result | Unlocks After |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Wood | 2 Driftwood | 1 Wood | Obtaining Driftwood |
| Plant Fiber | 5 Seaweed | 1 Plant Fiber | Obtaining Seaweed |
| String | 2 Plant Fiber | 1 String | Obtaining Plant Fiber |
| Cotton | 4 String | 1 Cotton | Obtaining String |

## Tools

| Recipe | Materials | Result | Unlocks After |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Sword | 2 Wood + 1 String + 4 Shark Teeth | Sword (Tier 1) | Obtaining Shark Teeth |
| Axe | 2 Wood + 1 String + 4 Shark Teeth | Axe (Tier 1) | Obtaining Shark Teeth |

## Components

| Recipe | Materials | Result | Unlocks After |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Wheel | 1 Broken Wheel + 1 Wood | 1 Wheel | Obtaining Broken Wheel |
| Sail | 5 Cotton + 5 Metal Scraps | 1 Sail | Obtaining Cotton |

## Major Upgrades

| Recipe | Materials | Result | Unlocks After |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Sailboat | 1 Sail + 1 Wheel + 20 Metal Scraps + 10 Wood | Sailboat | Obtaining Sail |

## Progression Chain

```
Seaweed (5) -> Plant Fiber (2) -> String (4) -> Cotton
                                      |
Driftwood (2) -> Wood ----------------+
                   |
Shark Teeth (4) --+-- String + Wood -> Sword/Axe
                   |
Broken Wheel -----+-- Wood -> Wheel
                                |
Metal Scraps (5) + Cotton (5) -> Sail
                                   |
Sail + Wheel + Metal Scraps (20) + Wood (10) -> Sailboat
```
